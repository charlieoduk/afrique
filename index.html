About my holiday spaces

Established in 2016 in Australia, My Holiday Spaces has grown from a small start-up to one of the middle travel e-commerce companies in the world. We now employ over 33 employees in two offices in two countries worldwide.

With a mission to empower people to travel and experience the world, we invest in digital technology that helps take the friction out of travel. At My Holiday Spaces , we connect travellers with the world's largest selection of incredible places to stay, including everything from apartments, vacation homes, and family-run B&Bs to 5-star luxury resorts, tree houses and even igloos.

My Holiday Spaces is also an affiliate of Booking.com. Each day, more than 1,550,000 room nights are booked on our platform. So whether travelling for business or leisure, customers can instantly book their ideal accommodation quickly and easily with My Holiday Spaces, without booking fees and backed up by our promise to price match.

Via our customer experience team, customers can reach My Holiday Spaces 24/7 for assistance, anytime of the day or night.
